About me

Hello, My name is Rasmus Andersson, and I am a 19-year-old high school student studying IT at NTI Gymnasiet in Helsingborg. As graduation is approaching, I am currently looking for work. During my years at NTI, I have worked with website development, setting up servers and routers, and building and installing computers. I have also worked with flowcharts and programmed in HTML/CSS, JavaScript, Java, C#, and Web-server SQL. During my final year, we have focused more on customer service and IT support, and I really enjoy that type of work. During my most recent internship, I worked for 8 weeks at Kullagatan Elektronik Service. There, I was responsible for greeting customers and explaining the business model, handling phone calls, and making initial assessments of technical issues. In addition to this, I worked on repairing products such as phones, computers, consoles, and more.

During my education, I have gained a solid understanding of IT and technology, which has provided me with a problem-solving mindset. I am someone who enjoys working both independently and in teams—skills I have developed through both my studies and personal interests. In my spare time, I enjoy playing computer games with friends, which has taught me how to collaborate and communicate effectively. I also appreciate being out in nature and hiking, which has given me both stamina and patience. I am responsible, thorough, and enjoy learning new things. Regardless of the task, I am committed and ensure the job is done well. During my internship, I received a lot of praise for being a fast learner and for absorbing information quickly enough to work independently. I hope to have the opportunity to contribute to your team and look forward to telling you more about myself in a potential interview.
